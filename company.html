What is BoxIT?

BoxIT Services is a management consulting firm & digital agency that helps business owners like yours do three things:

1. Develop strategies to reach customers
2. Get your finances in order, set you up for growth
3. Streamline your daily operations, get you running faster









About the founder

Dwight DeBacker
CEO

Dwight DeBacker is a seasoned management consulting expert & entrepreneur with over 30 years of industry experience.

Dwight founded BoxIT Services because he found a gap in the consulting services industry that underserved small & medium businesses; yet, he recognized the potential in this bulk of American entrepreneurship.

Dwight hopes to bring technology, efficacy, and efficiency to the world of small & medium businesses - and for this, he has brought together a diverse team of entrepreneurs to make it happen.
